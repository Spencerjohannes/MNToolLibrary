Access Over Ownership

The Minnesota Tool Library’s mission is to empower each other, reduce waste and improve communities by sharing tools, trade skills, and shop space.

Membership

Join the movement! You'll save money and make a difference in the process. We have a variety of membership levels to choose from!

Get Involved

There’s tons of ways to become a part of our community! Between classes, events, and volunteering, there’s something for everyone!
